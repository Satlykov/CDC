About Us

Coordination dispatch center "Energy" is an international non-government non-profit organization that implements round-the-clock dispatch management of the Central Asia Power System (CAPS).

CAPS consists of the power systems of Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan and southern zone of Kazakhstan, operating in conjunction and coordinated by dispatcher of CDC.

Also, our organization solves the problems of ensuring the reliable operation of the association with the observance of the parameters of the quality of electricity, and also ensures the mutually beneficial operation of energy systems as part of the CAPS.

CDC has been operating since 1960.
